Attack spells encompass all forms of direct magical combat, like shooting lightning, throwing a fireball or causing necrosis on a targets body.

Sustained: No

Base Spell: You attack a single target within short range with a beam of magical energy, dealing damage equal to your Willpower. Treat the spell like a ranged attack, applying ranged defenses, soak and environmental modifiers as usual. The attack has no Critical rating, and can only inflict a Critical Injury by spending t.

Base Difficulty: Easy(k)
