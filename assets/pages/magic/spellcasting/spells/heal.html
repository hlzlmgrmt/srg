Healing can remove damage or strain from a character, cure toxins and diseases, or allow to shut deaths door for anyone seeing the light. Additionally, healing spells can temporarily relieve the negative effects af allergies and addictions. Typically, healing spells are more difficult to cast than similat Medicine skill checks, but are often more versatile and quicker in their use. Generally, boosts and setbacks that apply to Medicine skill checks also apply for Healing spells.

Sustained: No

Base Spell: You choose one target that is Engaged with you and who is not incapacitated. Upon a success of the Spellcasting check, the target heals 1 wound per uncancelled s and 1 strain per uncanceled a.

Base Difficulty: Easy(k) + j for every 2 points of Essence Loss in the target.
