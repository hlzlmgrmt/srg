Tap into Qi: As a maneuver, an adept can commit one l from their Qi-Magic dice pool and choose a non-combat skill with the Brawn or Agility characteristic. While they commit this die, they add l to the chosen skill. generated on skill checks may be spend to add s or a to the result of the check. generated on the check may be used to suffer one strain, and then add s or a to the result of the check. Uncancelled h or t from checks boosted by l can be used to trigger effects from the table "Using h / d on Magic skill checks.". Unless otherwise specified, only a single l can be committed per skill, but multiple l can be committed to different skills simultaneously.

Use Adept Power: Adepts have access to unique, supernatural talents, called Adept Powers. These powers usually require the adept to commit l to use them. Adept powers count towards the talent pyramid; additionally to the usual restrictions on talents, adepts must at least possess a Qi-Magic rating equal to the desired talent.
