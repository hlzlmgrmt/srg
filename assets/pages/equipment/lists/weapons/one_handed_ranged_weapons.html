One-handed ranged weapons, such as tasers, pistols and mini-crossbows are light weapons, either primarily used as sidearms for non-combat heavy scenarios or utilized for more inconspicuous work. Characters using one-handed ranged weapons use the Ranged (Light) skill.

Light Pistol

Commonly seen in the pockets of city-dwellers, light pistols cover about any civilian-issued handgun.

Models Include: Ares Light Fire 70, Beretta 21IT, Colt America L26, Fichetti Executive Action

Heavy Pistol

The bigger, meaner brother of the pistol family. The heavy pistol utilizes bigger cartridges than its’ civilian counterparts (or even most SMGs, for that matter) and therefore packs a bigger punch than most other handguns. Shadowrunners, Corporate Agents and Drugged-Up Gang-Bangers alike favor these weapons as their main weapons in close quarters. Unlike their lighter counterparts, heavy pistols often require licenses and are not always completely legal to buy and carry.

Models Include: Colt Government 2066, Browning Ultra-Power, Ares Predator V

Holdout Pistol

Hold-out pistols are very cheap and concealable, making them perfect for sneaking into a meet or wearing as a back-up piece. Street-scum often carry these as a self-defense weapon. They don’t have much else going for them.

Models Include: Streetline Special, Fianchetti Tiffany Self-Defender 2075

Taser

This pacifists' weapon of choice fires small needles, that deliver a large electric shock through the attached wires into the unfortunate victim. Security forces, civilians and runners alike tend to carry these in order to non-lethally incapacitate their targets. Generally speaking, tasers are completely legal in almost all jurisdictions and surprisingly effective against armored targets, but require the re-cocking of the weapon after every shot. Don’t expect gangers to carry these.

Models Include: Defiance EX-Shocker, Yamaha Pulsar, Tiffany-Defiance Protector

Mini-Crossbow

Originally developed as a weapon for silent assassinations, various models of dart-shooters and pistol crossbows have hit the market after the widespread success of the Sliver. Surprisingly, due to the simple construction of a crossbow, the mini-crossbow sees widespread use as a lightweight sidearm among scuba divers and subnautical combat divers.

Mini-Crossbows are silent, adding jjj to all Perception and Vigilance checks made to locate a concealed shooter. Mini-Crossbows also do not suffer penalties from being fired underwater, in zero-air- or zero-gravity environments. Mini-Crossbows must be reloaded after every shot, and can load a wide variety of arrowheads.

Models Include: Ares Silverlight II, Ranger Sliver Pistol Crossbow

Dart Pistol

Parashield and Narcoject have released a series of weapons that defined the industry strandard for non-lethal dart pistols. Usually carried as a specialized sidearm of special forces teams in hostage situations, dart pistols shoot injection darts that deliver their payload on successful impact. Usually, that payload consists of a strong narcotic agent, but these darts can be filled with all kinds of toxins and drugs, if one so desires.

Dart pistols can never deal more than one wound, and do never case any Critical Injuries by themselves; instead, when a successful ranged combat check with the dart pistol delivers a wound to the target, the dart administers its’ payload.
