Brawling weapons are used to improve on hand-to-hand combat. They are usually used with the Brawl skill against a target within Engaged (kk) range. Unless otherwise noted (or at the Gamemasters discretion), the hands of someone wielding a Brawl weapon stays free, enabling them to interact with objects.
