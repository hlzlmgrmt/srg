Before spending any Karma during character creation, a character may choose five of their archetype skills and gain one rank in each of them. The rank of there skills cannot be raised above 2 at character creation. Archetype skills can be noted with a small • next to the skill.

List of Archetypes

Merc

The Mercs' base archetype skills are Athletics, Medicine, Pilot, Resilience, Vigilance and Ranged (Heavy).

Brawler

Brawlers add Brawl, Knowledge (Street), Melee and Qi-Magic to their list of archetype skills. Additionally, they must spend an additional 15 Karma before spending it on any other skills or talents.

Cyberadept

Cyberadepts add Discipline, Knowledge (Society), Ranged (Light) and Resonance to their list of archetype skills. Additionally, they must spend an additional 15 Karma before spending it on any other skills or talents.

Enforcer

Enforcers add Brawl, Coercion, Knowledge(Street) and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Gunner

Gunners add Coordination, Gunnery, Leadership and Mechanics to their list of archetype skills.

Spellslinger

Spellslingers add Brawl, Coercion, Knowledge (Street) and Spellcasting to their list of archetype skills. Additionally, they must spend an additional 15 Karma before spending it on any other skills or talents.

Street Samurai

Street Samurais add Discipline, Melee, Knowledge (Street) and Ranged (Light) to their list of archetype skills.

Decker

The Deckers' base archetype skills are Computers, Hacking, Knowledge (Society), Mechanics, Operating and Perception.

Datathief

Datathieves add Knowledge (Corporate), Ranged (Light), Skulduggery and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Hacktivist

Hacktivists add Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (Street), Leadership, and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Hammerhead

Hammerheads add Athletics, Melee, Ranged (Heavy) and Resilience to their list of archetype skills.

Investigative Journalist

Investigative Journalists add Deception, Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (History) and Resilience to their list of archetype skills.

Face

The Faces' base archetype skills are Charm, Deception, Knowledge (Society), Leadership, Negotiation and Vigilance.

Broker

Brokers add Computers, Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (Street) and Streetwise to theis list of archetype skills.

Exec

Execs add Coercion, Discipline, Ranged (Light) and Knowledge (Corporate to their list of archetype skills.

Punk

Punks add Coercion, Cool, Melee and Ranged (Heavy) to their list of archetype skills.

Smuggler

Smugglers add Knowledge (Corporate), Ranged (Light), Pilot and Survival to their list of archetype skills.

Specialist

The specialists' base archetype skills are Computers, Knowledge (Education), Knowledge (Corporate), Discipline, Negotiation and Vigilance.

Arcane Investigator

Arcane Investigators add Astral, Deception, Knowledge (Magic) and Spellcasting to their list of archetype skills. Additionally, they must spend an additional 30 Karma before spending it on any other skills or talents.

Agent

Agents add Deception, Ranged (Light), Skulduggery and Stealth to their list of archetype skills.

Corporate Liquidators

Corporate Liquidators add Cool, Coordination, Ranged (Heavy) and Stealth to their list of archetype skills.

Netshaman

Scientist

Scientists add Knowledge (History), Knowledge (Magic), Leadership and Perception to their list of archetype skills.

Survivalist

Rigger

The riggers' base archetype skills are Coordination, Gunnery, Mechanics, Navigation, Operating and Vigilance

Drone Rigger

Drone Riggers add Computers, Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (Education) and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Saboteur

Street Racer

Street Racers add Cool, Knowledge (Society), Knowledge (Street) and Pilot to their list of archetype skills.

Rules for Creating Custom Archetypes

When creating an archetype, each character gains ten archetype skills. These skills are associated with your characters general career, and taking ranks in these are generally cheaper than taking ranks in non-archetype skills.

When creating a custom Archetype for your character, choose 6 general skills that define the general archetype of the character. Then, choose 4 more skills, and you...

  • ...must have at least 1 social or combat skill,
  • ...must have at least 1 knowledge skill,
  • ...can’t have more than 3 combat skills (Hacking, Spellcasting, Summoning and Resonance all count as a combat skill),
  • ...can’t have more than 3 social skills,
  • ...can’t have more than 4 knowledge skills,
  • ...can’t have more than 2 additional general skills

These skills broadly represent a specialization of your character within its base archetype.

Magical skills have some further restrictions: When taking a magical skill as an archetype skill, you must pay 15 Karma for each skill, and you cannot take Resonance as a magical archetype skill if you have taken any other magical archetype skill. Additionally, when gaining any Magical skill except Resonance, your character gains the Awakened trait, or the Emergent trait if Resonance was taken as an archetype skill.
