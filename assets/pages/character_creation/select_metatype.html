Humans

2 2 2 2 2 2
Brawn Reflexes Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence

Starting Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Karma: 110

Starting Skills: Humans start with one rank in each of two different non-archetype skills at character creation. They obtain this rank before spending Karma, and these skills may not be increased higher than rank 2 during character creation.

Lucky: At the beginning of the session, each Human character turns one Story Point into an Edge. Once per session, a human adversaries with the Adversary talent may use one Story Point without awarding the Player Group an Edge.

Starting Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn

Orks

3 2 2 2 1 2
Brawn Reflexes Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence

Starting Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Starting Karma: 100

Starting Skills: Orks start with one rank in either Athletics or Survival, which they gain before spending any Karma and which cannot be raised higher than rank 2 during character creation.

Low-Light Vision: Orks can remove j from any check due to dim light.

Trog: When interacting with civilians, Orks add j to all Charm, Deception, Leadership, and Negotiation checks, but add j to all Coercion checks they make. This does not apply when interacting with other orks, trolls or members of the dubious circles of society.

Dwarfs

2 1 2 2 3 2
Brawn Reflexes Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence

Starting Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 11 + Willpower
Starting Karma: 95

Starting Skills: Dwarfs start with one rank in either Resilience or Discipline, which they gain before spending any Karma and which cannot be raised higher than rank 2 during character creation.

Therm-Optic Vision: Dwarfs can remove jj from any check due to dim light and total darkness, but suffer j to any check made in hot environments.

Pathological Resistance: Dwarfs gain an additional j to resist or recovery from poisons, toxins, venoms and diseases.

Stumpy: Dwarfs must take an additional maneuver when moving between Short and Medium range bands. They also receive j to all checks when using equipment not adjusted to their statue.

Note: Usually, weapons, armor, vehicles or other equipment are tailored and adjusted for dwarven characters when bought on the open market, and with a little bit of time, adjustments are easily made to accommodate them. However, equipment looted from other sources, like stolen weapons or vehicles, usually aren’t.

Brawn Reflexes Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence

Starting Wound Threshold: 14 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Karma: 65

Starting Skills: Trolls start with one rank in Coercion, which they gain before spending any karma and which cannot be raised higher than rank 2 during character creation.

Therm-Optic Vision: Trolls can remove jj from any check due to dim light and total darkness, but suffer j to any check made in hot environments.

Thick Skin: Trolls gain an additional point of Soak.

Trog: When interacting with civilians, Trolls add j to all Charm, Deception, Leadership, and Negotiation checks, but add j to all Coercion checks they make. This does not apply when interacting with other orks, trolls or members of the dubious circles of society.

Bulky: Due to their large statue, Trolls have a silhouette rating of 2. They decrease the Cumbersome rating of any weapon the wield by one. They also receive j to all checks when using equipment not adjusted to their statue.

Note: Usually, weapons, armor, vehicles or other equipment are tailored and adjusted for trollish characters when bought on the open market, and with a little bit of time, adjustments are easily made to accommodate them. However, equipment looted from other sources, like stolen weapons or vehicles, usually aren’t.
