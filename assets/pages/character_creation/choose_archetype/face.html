The Faces' base archetype skills are Charm, Deception, Knowledge (Society), Leadership, Negotiation and Vigilance.

Broker

Brokers add Computers, Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (Street) and Streetwise to theis list of archetype skills.

Exec

Execs add Coercion, Discipline, Ranged (Light> and Knowledge (Corporate to their list of archetype skills.

Punk

Punks add Coercion, Cool, Melee and Ranged (Heavy) to their list of archetype skills.

Smuggler

Smugglers add Knowledge (Corporate), Ranged (Light), Pilot and Survival to their list of archetype skills.
