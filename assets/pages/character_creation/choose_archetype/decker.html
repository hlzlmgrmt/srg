The Deckers' base archetype skills are Computers, Hacking, Knowledge (Society), Mechanics, Operating and Perception.

Datathief

Datathieves add Knowledge (Corporate), Ranged (Light), Skulduggery and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Hacktivist

Hacktivists add Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (Street), Leadership, and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Hammerhead

Hammerheads add Athletics, Melee, Ranged (Heavy) and Resilience to their list of archetype skills.

Investigative Journalist

Investigative Journalists add Deception, Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (History) and Resilience to their list of archetype skills.
