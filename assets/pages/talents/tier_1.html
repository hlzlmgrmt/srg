Tier 1 talents are usually simple talents with small or situational effects. Ranked talents that increase your wound and strain thresholds by a small amount per rank, that reduce j or that add j in specific situations belong to this tier. Additionally, talents that allow your character to convert maneuvers into incidentals - talents like Quick Draw - belong to this tier.
