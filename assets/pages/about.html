The Shadowrun Genesys project is a one-to six man project that aims to reduce the crunch of the Shadowrun TTRPGs and minimize them into a simpler, yet similarily exciting format. Ports of Shadowrun for the Genesys and GURPS systems already exist, but they often keep the bloated complexity of the original system, resulting in hundreds of pages of tables and sub-rules of sub-rules that make switching to those ports difficult.
