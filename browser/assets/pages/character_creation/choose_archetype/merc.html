The Mercs' base archetype skills are Athletics, Medicine, Pilot, Resilience, Vigilance and Ranged (Heavy).

Brawler

Brawlers add Brawl, Knowledge (Street), Melee and Qi-Magic to their list of archetype skills. Additionally, they must spend an additional 15 Karma before spending it on any other skills or talents.

Cyberadept

Cyberadepts add Discipline, Knowledge (Society), Ranged (Light) and Resonance to their list of archetype skills. Additionally, they must spend an additional 15 Karma before spending it on any other skills or talents.

Enforcer

Enforcers add Brawl, Coercion, Knowledge(Street) and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Gunner

Gunners add Coordination, Gunnery, Leadership and Mechanics to their list of archetype skills.

Spellslinger

Spellslingers add Brawl, Coercion, Knowledge (Street) and Spellcasting to their list of archetype skills. Additionally, they must spend an additional 15 Karma before spending it on any other skills or talents.

Street Samurai

Street Samurais add Discipline, Melee, Knowledge (Street) and Ranged (Light) to their list of archetype skills.
