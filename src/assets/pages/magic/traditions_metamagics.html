The term "Metamagic" describes a technique utilized by Awakened magicians, summoners and adepts which have attained a higher understanding of magic through initiation. Metamagics allow an Awakened to manipulate and utilize their magical powers in more powerful ways unmatched by their uninitiated peers.

Metamagics are - at their core - talents, which differ in two ways from ordinary magical talents. First, metamagic talents are limited for an individual Awakened. In order ot gain a Metamagic, a character must gain a rank in the Magic Initiate talent; since the talent is a Tier 2 talent, and no ranked talent may be taken higher than 5, any magician can only gain a total of four different metamagics without the aid of specialized equipment, such as metamagic foci. This makes metamagics a specialized skillset, that can be useful in differentiating traditions from each other.

Second, metamagics mainly utilize the Awakened character's ranks in Magic Initiate instead of skill ranks. This makes almost all metamagics independent from their actual skills, and makes each individual metamagic stronger with each additional metamagic gained.
