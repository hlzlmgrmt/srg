Augment spells magically enhance characters or objects, imbuing swords with magical power or enhancing a characters reflexes. Augment spells generally only work on voluntary targets, and cannot be in detriment to the target. Objects augmented through magical means are usually simple objects, such as swords, or simple metal alloys; enhancing a gun or drone often proves much more difficult.

Sustained: Yes

Base Spell: You select a skill using the Brawn or Reflexes characteristic, and a voluntary target engaged with you. If successful, as long as the spell is sustained, the target increases the ability of all skill checks made with the chosen skill once (in effect, this means they add one [[a]] to their checks). A character cannot be the target of multiple Augment spells at the same time.

Base Difficulty: Average([[dd]])

Additional Effects:

Effect Diff. Mod.

Additional Target: The spell affects one additional target within range of the spell. In addition, after casting the spell, you may spend [(a)] to affect oe additional target within range of the spell (and may trigger this multiple times, spending [(a)] each time).

+[[d]]

Enchant: The spell affects a Melee weapon a character within the range of the spell is holding. While the spell is sustained, in addition to other effects, the weapon increases their damage by one, and reduces their Critical rating by one, to a minimum of 2. The augmented weapon becomes magical, and may ignore an adversaries immunity to non-magical attacks.

 +[[d]]

Mental: You may augment any non-magical skill of a target.

+[[d]]

Range: Increase the range of the spell by one range band. This may be added multiple times, increasing the range by one range band each time (to a maximum of Long range).

+[[d]]

Swift: Targets affected by the spell ignore the effects of difficult terrain, and they cannot be Immobilized.
