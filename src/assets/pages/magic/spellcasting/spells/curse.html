Curses inflict negative afflictions and effects to targets without causing direct harm, wether through direct penalties to checks made by the cursed target, sicknesses that ravage their bodies or the illusion of excruciating pain. In combat, most curses are simple debuffs that hinder adversaries in their ability to do things. Outside of combat, curses can be longer lasting and more deliberate; someone could be forced to vomit, for example.

Sustained: Yes

Base Spell: You weaken a target within short range with a hex. If successful, as long as the spell is sustained, the target decreases the ability of all skill checks they make by one (in effect, this means they remove one k from their checks). Once per turn, as a maneuver, or once per minute, the target of a curse can attempt to break the spell by succeeding a Resilience skill check as a Contested check against your Spellcasting skill.

Base Difficulty: Average(kk)
