Sustained: Yes

Base Spell: You can target a non-living target with an Encumbrance equal or lower than your ranks in Spellcasting within short range. If the spell is successful, you can move the target by one range band in any direction (This counts as one maneuver worth of movement). While you are sustaining the spell, you can spend a maneuver to move the target by one range band in any direction up to medium range, or two maneuvers to move the target from medium to long range or reversed. While affected by the spell, the target is rolling or sliding in the chosen direction, and is slow enough to be caught while moving.

Base Difficulty: Simple (-)
