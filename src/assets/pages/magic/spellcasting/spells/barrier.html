Some spellcasters have the power to create barriers of magical energy to protect themselves and their allies. "Barriers," or protective, spells are fairly straightforward in structured encounters: they reduce incoming damage for your character and their allies. However, they have some interesting narrative uses as well. Barriers let the spellcaster protect themselves from adverse conditions of all types. Your character may create a bubble under the ocean so they can travel underwater, a barrier that shields them from flames as they walk through a burning building, or even a barrier that deflects an onrushing avalanche.
