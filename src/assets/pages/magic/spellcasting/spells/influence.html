Some spellcasters have learned to use their magic to influence the minds, emotions and even the body of other people. These spells are one of the main reasons for the mayor distrust many mages face in society, as the masters of mind altering magicks are able to force their victims to act against their own will. While some magicians favor to influence their victims through their magic in order to avoid conflict, the news are filled with countless atrocities committed by the vicims of mind-control magic. Some use of Influence can even be so subtile, that a victim does not even recognize being manipulated at all, adding up to the paranoia.

Sustained: Yes

Base Spell: You influence the mind of a target that is engaged with you, making it easier for them to agree with you. If the spell is successful, while you sustain the spell, you add [[s]] to all opposed dice pools of Social skill checks you make against your spells' target. The effects of the spell end when you stop sustaining the spell, and while your target does not necessarily knows that they have been hexed, they feel like they have been fed honeyed words.

Base Difficulty: Easy([[d]])
