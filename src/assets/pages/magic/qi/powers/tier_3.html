Combat Sense

Tier: 3
Activation: Active (Maneuver)

You may suffer two strain and commit [[p]]; while you use the Combat Sense power, you gain one defense. Additionally, while Combat Sense is active, you may add [[w]] to all checks to spot adversaries that are actively hiding or trying to ambush you.

Killing Hands (Improved)

Tier: 3
Activation: Passive
Prerequisites: Killing Hands Adept power

You can activate the Killing Hands adept power as an Incidental. Additionally, while you use the Killing Hands adept power, you may use the Tap Into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to all Brawl checks.

Kinesics (Improved)

Tier: 3
Activation: Passive

While you use the Kinesics adept power, you can communicate with other people using incredibly subtle body language. While other people cannot directly answer to your communication, other adepts with the Kinesics (Improved) adept power can hold a conversation with you. Onlookers must succeed an opposed Vigilance check against your Qi-Magic dice pool in order to notice that you are using this power.

Reflect (Improved)

Tier: 3
Activation: Active (Incidental, Out-Of-Turn)
Ranked: No
Prerequisite: Reflect adept power

When your character suffers a hit from a ranged combat check and uses Deflect to reduce the damage from that hit, after the attack is resolved, you may spend [(d)] or [(hhh)] to automatically hit one adversary within short range. The hit deals the same damage as the initial ranged attack.

The initial ranged attack's hit must be one that can be reflected and redirected (generally only bullets, knifes, grenades or similar). This talent may not be used if the original attack incapacitates the character.

Uncanny Reflexes (Improved)

Tier: 3
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Prerequisites: Uncanny Reflexes adept power

When you use the Tap into Qi maneuver in order to add [[w]] to you initiative, you can suffer two stain and commit [[pp]]; until the end of the encounter, you gain an additional, free maneuver. Additionally, once per turn, you may suffer two strain to gain a third maneuver. You can activate this power after you have already rolled for initiative; doing so takes an action instead of an incidental, and you cannot use your free maneuver in the same turn this power was activated as an action.
