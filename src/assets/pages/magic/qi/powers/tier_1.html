Tier 1 Adept Powers are usually simple powers that adept often buy en-masse in order to diversify their skills. These talents include additional skills for Tap into Qi-maneuvers, or provide benefits that low-tier cyberware provides.

Authority

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to your Coercion skill checks.

Danger Sense

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to all Vigilance checks in order to avoid traps and hazards.

Killing Hands

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Maneuver)

You can suffer two strain and commit [[p]] from your Qi-Magic dice pool; until the end of the encounter, you can increase the damage of your unarmed attacks by one, and set their Critical rating to 3. Additionally, you can choose to ignore the Nonlethal quality when attacking with your bare hands, and attacks made with them become magical.

Uncanny Analytics

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to all skill checks made to analyze, detect, recognize, and solve ciphers, patterns and puzzles.

Uncanny Reflexes

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

When you make a skill check in order to determine initiative, you can use the Tap into Qi maneuver as an incidental to add [[w]] to the roll.
