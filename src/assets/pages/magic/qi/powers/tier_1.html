Tier 1 Adept Powers are usually simple powers that adept often buy en-masse in order to diversify their skills. These talents include additional skills for Tap into Qi-maneuvers, or provide benefits that low-tier cyberware provides.

Analytics

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to all skill checks made to analyze, detect, recognize, and solve ciphers, patterns and puzzles.

Authority

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to your Coercion skill checks.

Danger Sense

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to all checks in order to avoid traps, hazards and dangerous situations.

Direction Sense

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to all Navigation checks to orient yourself in the (urban) wilderness.

Killing Hands

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Maneuver)

You can suffer two strain and commit [[p]] from your Qi-Magic dice pool; until the end of the encounter, you can increase the damage of your unarmed attacks by one, and set their Critical rating to 3. Additionally, you can choose to ignore the Nonlethal quality when attacking with your bare hands, and attacks made with them become magical.

Mystic Armor

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Maneuver)

You can suffer two strain and commit [[p]] from your Qi-Magic dice pool; until the end of the scene or encounter, you gain one soak.

Nightvision

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Maneuver)

You can suffer two strain and commit [[p]] from your Qi-Magic dice pool; until the end of the scene or encounter, you remove [[s]] from checks due to dim lights.

Pain Resistance

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Incidental, Out-Of-Turn)

You can suffer two strain and commit [[p]] from your Qi-Magic dice pool; until the end of the scene or encounter, you can reduce the difficulty of all Resilience and Discipline checks to resist pain or physical coercion by one.

Purity

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Incidental, Out-Of-Turn)

You can suffer two strain and commit [[p]] from your Qi-Magic dice pool; until the end of the scene or encounter, you can reduce the difficulty of all Resilience checks to resist poisons, toxins, diseases or pollution by one.

Rapid Healing

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap Into Qi maneuver for all Medicine checks made to recover from wounds or strain, or to heal your Critical Injuries. This also applies to checks made by allies to heal your character, but only your character.

Uncanny Reflexes

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

When you make a skill check in order to determine initiative, you can use the Tap into Qi maneuver as an incidental to add [[w]] to the roll.

Wall Running

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive

You can use the Tap into Qi maneuver to add [[w]] to all Athletic checks in order to run, jump and climb. Additionally, while you use Wall Running, you count as having a climbing tool for up to Medium range in any direction.
