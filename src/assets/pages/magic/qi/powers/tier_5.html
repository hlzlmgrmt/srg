Attribute Boost

Tier: 5
Activation: Active (Maneuver)

When you activate this power, you can choose a characteristic, suffer strain equal to your chosen characteristic, plus one, and commit [[p]] equal to half of your chosen characteristic, plus one, rounded up, from your Qi-Magic pool ([[p]] when your chosen characteristic is 1, [[pp]] when it's 2 or 3, or [[ppp]] when it's 4 or 5). Until the end of the encounter or scene, you increase your chosen characteristic by one. You cannot increase your chosen characteristic above 6 by using the Attribute Boost power, and you do not change any derived attributes (wound or strain threshold, soak etc.) by using this talent.

Uncanny Reflexes (Supreme)

Tier: 5
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Prerequisites: Uncanny Reflexes (Improved) adept power

When you use the Tap into Qi maneuver in order to add [[w]] to you initiative, you can commit [[ppp]] from your Qi-Magic dice pool; until the end of the encounter, as an incidental at the end of a round, you can suffer three strain to use an additional action.
