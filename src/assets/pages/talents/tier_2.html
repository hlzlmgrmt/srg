Tier 2 talents often encompass talents that change how a character is going to behave throuought an encounter or a scene. In combat, talents that reliably apply defenses and that give character more combat options fit this tier, as well as talents that provide minor, but useful bonuses to their allies. Overall, Tier 2 talents are useful enough for most characters to take, without them becoming an "auto-include" on every character.

Berserk

Tier: 2
Activation: Active (Maneuver)
Ranked: No

Once per encounter, until the end of the encounter or until your character in incapacitated, your character adds saa to all Brawl and Melee combat checks they make. However, opponents targeting your character add s to all combat checks. While berserk, your character cannot make ranged combat checks. At the end of the encounter (or when you become incapacitated), your character suffers 6 strain.

Burly

Tier: 2
Activation: Passive
Ranked: Yes

Each rank in Burly reduces the Cumbersome quality and the Encumbrance rating of any weapon you are wielding by one, to a minimum of 1.

Finesse

Tier: 2
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No

When making a Brawl or Melee check with a weapon with a maximum encumbrance rating of 1, you may use your Agility instead of Brawn for the check.
