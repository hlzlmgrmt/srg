Magic Tradition (Improved)

Tier: 3
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No
Prerequisites: Magic Tradition talent

When you choose this talent, choose a non-combat and non-magical skill that has a characteristic that matches the characteristic you chose for the Magical Tradition talent. When you build your dice pool with your chosen characteristic, and choose an additional effect that adds your ranks in a magical skill to a resulting effect, you may use your ranks of your chosen skill instead, and if you do, you may add your ranks of your chosen characteristic.

Mentor Spirit

Tier: 3
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No

When you choose this talent, you can choose a Mentor Spirit.

More Machine Than Man

Tier: 3
Activation: Passive
Ranked: Yes

Increase your characters Essence loss threshold by 1 for each rank in More Machine Then Man.
