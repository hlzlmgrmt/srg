Magic Tradition

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No Prerequisites: Awakened trait

When taking this talent, you may choose between one of the following characteristics: Brawn, intellect, Cunning or Presence. Once per session, when you suffer strain due to using a Magical skill, you may ignore an amount of strain equal to your chosen characteristic.

Matrix Search

Tier: 1
Activation: Passive
Ranked: No

When you or one of your allies make a Knowledge skill check, you may use your Computers skill instead of Knowledge to roll or aid in the roll. This tallen cannot be used if you are offline, or if you dont have at least half an hour of time to conduct research through the net.

Quick Draw

Tier: 1
Activation: Active (Incidental)
Ranked: No

Once per round of your character's turn, you may use this talent to draw or holster an easily accessible weapon or item as an incidental. Quick Draw also also reduces a weapon's Prepare rating by one, to a minimum of zero.
