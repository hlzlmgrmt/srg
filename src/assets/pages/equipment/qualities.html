Some equipment features special qualities that add variety and depth to the massive array of weapons, armor, and items that exist in the Sixth World. Some qualities are inherent so certain items, in order to differentiate them from another outside of simple damage numbers, while others can be applied through modifications to specifically tailor a piece of equipment to someones liking.

Item qualities are generally either passive or active. Passive qualities are always "on" and require no activation on the part of the user. Active qualities must be triggered by the user, often by spending one or more a to activate the effect.

Item qualities often have a number associated with them. This is their rating. Ratings affect qualities in different ways, depending on the quality in question.

Active qualities require aa to activate unless otherwise stated in their description.
