Heavy ranged weapons are bulky pieces of equipment, trading combat utility for raw firepower. Usually, these weapons are designed to be bolted to vehicles or used with special harnesses, but exceptionally strong individuals (and trolls) are able to withstand the recoil of their bug guns without much effort. Characters using heavy ranged weapons use the Gunnery skill.

Assault Cannon

These babies are the very definition of the word "overkill". Simply put, Assault Cannons are miniaturized versions of tank cannons, and provide enough firepower to plow through entire blocks of houses. While some of the cheaper models use an ungodly amount of propellant, higher-end models tend to be designed as railguns; they accelerate tungsten metal rods through the use of magnetic rails.

Models Include: Krime Cannon, Panther XXL, Ares Thunderstruck Gauss Rifle
