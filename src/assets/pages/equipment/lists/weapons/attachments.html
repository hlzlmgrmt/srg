Attachable Stock

This attachment can be applied to all guns and crossbows without the Cumbersome qualiy, except Holdout Pistols and Tasers.

Base Modifier: As a maneuver, the stock can be attached or detached from the weapon. Two-handed ranged weapons replace their pre-built stock with this, and do not increase their Encumbrance rating due to this attachment

Modification Options: 1x Folding Stock (Attach or Detach stock as an Incidental) Mod, 1x Lightweight (Enc. -1) Mod, 1x Quality (HP +1) Mod, 1x Quality (Accurate +1) Mod

Suppressor

As the classic assassin’s accessory, suppressors allow a user to keep unwanted attention from themselves by supressing noise. Unlike the silencers often found in mainstream action trideos, suppressors do not make a gun magically imperceptible; If a suppressed weapon is fired inside a small enclosed room, people will notice the gunfire. Rather, a suppressor will distort the sound of gunfire to a point that people will not perceive it as such, especially when the sound comes from behind a bunch of walls. This additionally hinders witnesses of an assassination in locating the shooter in time.

Due to this, after-market attachments of suppressors are generally illegal, but more often than not, corporate security – especially those within arcologies – carry suppressed weapons to deal with criminals without enticing mass panic within the closed environment. Guns sold with integrated suppressors are therefore known and registered, and – with the right licenses of course – legal to buy and carry.

Suppressors come in two variants: Attached and integrated. Attached suppressors do not cost Hard Points, but are rather big and clunky, and therefor add one point of Encumbrance to the weapon while attached. Attaching a suppressor uses a maneuver, and getting caught with one is generally a bad idea. Integrated suppressors are built into the gun barrel itself. They cost a single Hard Point, but do not add to the weapons bulk. Spotting an inbuilt suppressor requires a Hard (kkk) Perception or Mechanics check.

This attachment can be applied to pistols, submachine guns, assault rifles, precision rifles and sniper rifles.

Base Modifiers: Adds jj to all Perception or Vigilance checks made to locate a concealed shooter. Inbuild suppressors require a Hard (kkk) Perception or Mechanics check to be spotted.

Modification Options: 2x Improvement (Add additional j to Perception and Vigilance checks to spot shooter) Mod
