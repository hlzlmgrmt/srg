Armored Clothing

A bulletproof vest worn under a buttoned-up shirt, a kevlar-reinforced christmas sweater, or a very thick synth-leather jacket are all equally terrible at protecting you in a real gunfight, but they might be able to stop the occasional stray bullet down the block. Most city-dwellers wear these as everyday wear.

Armored Vest

Corporate police forces and battle-ready street scum wear these thick kevlar vests in an attempt to keep alive in the mean streets of the Sixth World.
