Characteristics

Brawn

A characters Brawn (BRA) represents a blend of pure brutish power, strength, and overall toughness. Characters with a high Brawn rating are usually physically fir and hardy, rarely get sick, and have strong constitutions.

Brawn is used in a number of physical skills, such as Athletics, Resilience, and Melee. Brawn is also used to determine your characters' total wound threshold.

Reflexes

The Reflexes characteristic (REF) measures your characters' manual dexterity, hand-eye coordination and overall body control. Characters with high reflexes are flexible, have deft hand and can instinctively adapt to sudden situations.

Reflexes are used for a number of physical skills, such as Coordination, Stealth, and Ranged Combat, but is also the main characteristic for Pilot.

Intellect

The Intellect characteristic (INT) measures your characters' intelligence, education, mental acuity, and ability to reason and rationalize. Characters with a high Intellect can extrapolate and interpolate data, can recall details and draw from previous experience and think of long-term strategies and envision the ramifications of present actions.

Intellect is used for a number of mental skills, including all Knowledge skills, Medicine, Mechanics, Computers, Hacking and Operating.

Cunning

Cunning (CUN) eflects how crafty, devious, clever, and creative your character can be. Characters with a high Cunning are savvy, quickly pick up on vital social and environmental clues, and can more readily come up with short-term plans and tactics.

Cunning is used for a number of mental skills, including Deception, Perception, Skulduggery, and Survival.

Willpower

The Willpower characteristic (WIL) reflects your character’s discipline, self-control, mental fortitude, and faith. Characters with a high Willpower can withstand stress and fatigue, remain composed during chaotic situations, and exert influence over the weaker willed. Awakened and Emergent characters, such as magicians, adepts and technomancers, tend to have high Willpower in order to influence the world around them and withstand the repercussions of their weird powers.

Willpower is used for skills such as Coercion and Vigilance, and is the key ability for all Magical skills. Willpower is also used to determine your character’s total strain threshold.

Presence

A character’s Presence (PRE) characteristic represents their moxie, charisma, confidence, and force of personality. Characters with a high Presence make natural leaders, draw attention when they enter a room, can easily strike up a conversation with nearly anyone, and are quick to adapt to social situations.

Presence is the key characteristic for interpersonal skills, such as Charm and Leadership.
