Simple cybernetic implants, such as small, implated datajacks, simple, color-changing cybereyes, LED tattoos or similar cosmetic enhancements can be acquired for almost no cost involved, either in money or in essence. Of course, most Awakened (and Emergent) metahumans are extremely cautious about their spiritual holisticity when deciding to install cybernetics, so purists tend to go without chrome; instead, most magicians will use old-school devices to interact with the Matrix (and look really stupid doing so). Rule of thumb regarding purely cosmetic cybernetics is: If it is all looks without grander function, and if it does not outlandishly alter the character's appearance too much, it does not require money or Essence loss.
