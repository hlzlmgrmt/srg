Starting Wound Threshold: 10 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 10 + Willpower
Starting Karma: 110

Starting Skills: Humans start with one rank in each of two different non-archetype skills at character creation. They obtain this rank before spending Karma, and these skills may not be increased higher than rank 2 during character creation.

Lucky: At the beginning of the session, each Human character turns one Story Point into an Edge. Once per session, a human adversaries with the Adversary talent may use one Story Point without awarding the Player Group an Edge.
