Starting Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 9 + Willpower
Starting Karma: 100

Starting Skills: Orks start with one rank in either Athletics or Survival, which they gain before spending any Karma and which cannot be raised higher than rank 2 during character creation.

Low-Light Vision: Orks can remove j from any check due to dim light.

Trog: When interacting with civilians, Orks add j to all Charm, Deception, Leadership, and Negotiation checks, but add j to all Coercion checks they make. This does not apply when interacting with other orks, trolls or members of the dubious circles of society.
