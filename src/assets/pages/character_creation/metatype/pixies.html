Starting Wound Threshold: 4 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 8 + Willpower
Starting Karma:

Starting Skills: Pixies start with one rank in Stealth and Spellcasting, which they gain before spending any Karma and which cannot be raised higher than rank 2 during character creation. Pixies gain the Awakened trait.

Flight: As part of their first rank in Spellcasting, Pixies gain the Move spell. When a pixie casts the Move spell on themselves in order to fly around, they reduce the difficulty of the spell by two, to a minimum of Simple (-).

Vanishing: Pixies gain one rank of the Expanded Grimoire talent, which they must spend to gain the Misdirect spell. If a pixie casts a Misdirect spell at themselves that makes them harder to notice or spot, they reduce the difficulty of the spell by two, to a minimum of Simple (-).

Small: Pixies have a silhouette rating of 0. Due to their small size, Pixies cannot use any equipment not explicitly tailored to their statue. Additionally, Pixies can never raise their Brawn rating beyond 1 without magical means.
