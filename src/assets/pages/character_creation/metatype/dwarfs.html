Brawn Reflexes Intellect Cunning Willpower Presence

Starting Wound Threshold: 12 + Brawn
Starting Strain Threshold: 11 + Willpower
Starting Karma: 95

Starting Skills: Dwarfs start with one rank in either Resilience or Discipline, which they gain before spending any Karma and which cannot be raised higher than rank 2 during character creation.

Therm-Optic Vision: Dwarfs can remove [[ss]] from any check due to dim light and total darkness, but suffer [[s]][[s]] to any check made in hot environments.

Pathological Resistance: Dwarfs gain an additional [[b]] to resist or recovery from poisons, toxins, venoms and diseases.

Stumpy: Dwarfs must take an additional maneuver when moving between Short and Medium range bands. They also receive [[s]] to all checks when using equipment not adjusted to their statue.

Note: Usually, weapons, armor, vehicles or other equipment are tailored and adjusted for dwarven characters when bought on the open market, and with a little bit of time, adjustments are easily made to accommodate them. However, equipment looted from other sources, like stolen weapons or vehicles, usually aren’t.
