The riggers' base archetype skills are Coordination, Gunnery, Mechanics, Navigation, Operating and Vigilance

Drone Rigger

Drone Riggers add Computers, Knowledge (Corporate), Knowledge (Education) and Streetwise to their list of archetype skills.

Saboteur

Saboteurs add Hacking, Knowledge (Corporate), Ranged (Heavy) and Resilience to their list of archetype skills.

Security Spider

Security Spiders add Discipline, Hacking, Leadership and Perception to their list of archetype skills.

Street Racer

Street Racers add Cool, Knowledge (Society), Knowledge (Street) and Pilot to their list of archetype skills.
